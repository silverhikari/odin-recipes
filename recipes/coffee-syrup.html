Coffee Syrup

Description

Coffee Syrup adds a delight taste of coffee to projects such as milkshakes, coffee milk, and more. While not hard to make, it can be time consuming as it take a while for it to boil down and condense.

Ingredients

  • 3 Cups Sugar or Sugar equivalent
  • 3 Cups Coffee (instant or brewed)

Cooking Steps

  1. brew your coffee or add your instant coffee powder to hot water
  2. mix sugar and coffee until dissolved in a large pot
  3. turn on burner on medium heat until strong boil
  4. lower to a medium-low heat and stir occasionally
  5. cook until mixture has decreased by half or your desired thickness
