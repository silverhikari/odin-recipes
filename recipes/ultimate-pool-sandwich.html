The Ultimate Pool Sandwich

Description

a stuffed sandwich that will not fall apart while eating or traveling!

filled with tasty Ingredients that can be customized to your liking, if you wish

Ingredients

  • 1 round bread loaf
  • 2 Tbs butter
  • 2 Tbs yellow mustard
  • 2 Cup Shredded iceberg lettuce
  • 1/2 lb sliced deli ham
  • 1/2 lb sliced deli turkey
  • 6 slices Swiss cheese
  • 6 slices Cheddar or Colby Jack cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350F (175C)
  2. cut a wide circle out of the bread loaf top as if you were making a soup bowl. store loaf top for later.
  3. hollow out the loaf, while make sure not to pierce the sides. the load interior can be used for other projects
  4. melt butter with mustard for 15 minutes in a bowl. mix after
  5. brush butter mixture on exterior of bread loaf and top. place both on a baking sheet
  6. bake in oven for 10 minutes. let cool to room temperature
  7. layer the inside of the Sandwich like so (starting from bottom to top):
    1. lettuce
    2. cheese
    3. ham
    4. turkey
    repeat until sandwich is full, place loaf top on top.
  8. wrap sandwich with plastic wrap, place on baking sheet while placing another sheet on top with weight
  9. place this structure in fridge, letting sit for 1 hour or overnight
  10. eat whole or cut into slices
