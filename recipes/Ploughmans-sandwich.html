Ploughman's Sandwich

Description

a traditional sandwich reminiscent of the laborer's midday break which gives the sandwich it's name.

a popular sandwich served in bars.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices crusty bread
  • 1 tsp butter, softened
  • handful of arugula
  • 2 sweet gherkin pickles, sliced
  • 3 slices smoked ham
  • 2 slices white cheddar cheese
  • 1 small tomato, sliced
  • 1 tsp whole grain mustard

Steps

  1. lightly butter both slices of bread
  2. layer sandwich with the rest of the Ingredients on one slice of bread. spread mustard on the other slice and place on top. cut in half and serve.
